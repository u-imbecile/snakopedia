Welcome to the world of snake.



Snakes are not evil; they are misunderstood.
Most of them are harmless and prefer to avoid
humans. Ancient myths turned them into villains,
but they are simply animals trying to survive.
Their cold stare is natural, as they do not
have eyelids to blink. People fear them
because of stories, not because of
facts. This site is here to reveal
the truth: snakes are vital, not vicious.
