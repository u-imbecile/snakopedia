Non-Venomous Snakes

Checkered Keelback
Checkered Keelback is a non-venomous water snake found near ponds and rivers. It feeds on fish and frogs and is often mistaken as dangerous.

Indian Rock Python
The Indian Rock Python is a large, non-venomous constrictor. It kills prey by wrapping around and suffocating them, not by venom.

Rat Snake
The Rat Snake is a fast, non-venomous snake often mistaken for a cobra. It helps control rodent populations in farms and homes.
